The loop

Sniff → Decide → Make → Gate → Ship → Learn

Marketing Dog runs a closed loop. Every cycle it gets a little smarter about what your audience actually rewards.

01
Sniff
Scans your site, products, trends & competitors. Extracts your palette, voice & logo.
02
Decide
The orchestrator scores demand × trend × audience and fills a calendar with reasons.
03
Make
Specialist agents draft posts, carousels, blogs, emails, reels — in your voice.
04
Gate
The Critic hard-blocks off-brand or cross-vertical content before a human ever sees it.
05
Ship
One-click native publishing to IG, YouTube, TikTok, X, LinkedIn, Pinterest, email.
06
Learn
Winners feed back into the brain. Next cycle scores higher. Repeat.
